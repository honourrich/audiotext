# Video Export Feature Guide

## Overview

The Video Export feature in audiotext allows you to create professional, social media-ready videos with hardcoded subtitles and branding overlays. This feature is perfect for content creators who want to repurpose their podcast episodes into engaging video content for platforms like TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and more.

## Key Features

- **Multi-Platform Support**: Export videos optimized for TikTok (9:16), Instagram (1:1), YouTube (16:9), and other social platforms
- **Automatic Subtitle Generation**: Convert your podcast transcripts into timed subtitles
- **Branding Overlays**: Add logos, watermarks, and text overlays to your videos
- **Aspect Ratio Conversion**: Automatically crop or pad videos to fit different platform requirements
- **Real-time Preview**: See exactly how your video will look before exporting
- **High-Quality Processing**: Powered by FFmpeg.wasm for professional video processing

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites

- A video file (MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WEBM)
- A podcast episode with transcript (optional)
- Logo or branding assets (optional)

### Supported Video Formats

- **Input**: MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WEBM
- **Output**: MP4 (H.264)
- **Maximum file size**: 500MB
- **Recommended resolution**: 1080p or higher

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Access Video Export

There are three ways to access the Video Export feature:

1. **From Dashboard**: Click "Upload" → "Video Export" tab
2. **From Episode Editor**: Open any episode → "Video" tab
3. **From Onboarding**: Complete the feature tour to learn about video export

### Step 2: Upload Your Video

1. **Select Video File**:
   - Drag and drop your video file onto the upload area
   - Or click "Browse" to select from your computer
   - Supported formats: MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WEBM

2. **Video Preview**:
   - Once uploaded, you'll see a preview of your video
   - Use the timeline to scrub through different parts
   - Check that the video quality and content look good

### Step 3: Configure Subtitles

1. **Use Episode Transcript** (Recommended):
   - If you're working with a podcast episode, click "Use Episode Transcript"
   - The system will automatically generate timed subtitles from your transcript
   - Review and edit the subtitle timing and text as needed

2. **Manual Subtitle Creation**:
   - Click "Add Subtitle" to create custom subtitles
   - Set start and end times for each subtitle
   - Edit the text content
   - Style the subtitles (font, size, color, background)

3. **Subtitle Styling**:
   - **Font**: Choose from available fonts
   - **Size**: Adjust subtitle size (recommended: 24-32px)
   - **Color**: Set text color (white or yellow work best)
   - **Background**: Add background for better readability
   - **Position**: Choose top, center, or bottom positioning

### Step 4: Add Branding Elements

1. **Upload Logo**:
   - Click "Upload Logo" to add your brand logo
   - Supported formats: PNG, JPG, SVG
   - Recommended size: 200x200px or larger

2. **Logo Positioning**:
   - Drag the logo to your desired position
   - Use the position controls for precise placement
   - Adjust opacity (recommended: 70-90% for subtle branding)

3. **Text Watermarks**:
   - Add text watermarks like "@yourhandle" or "Subscribe"
   - Choose font, size, and color
   - Position in corners or along edges

### Step 5: Select Output Format

1. **Choose Platform**:
   - **TikTok**: 1080x1920 (9:16) - Vertical format for TikTok and Instagram Reels
   - **Instagram Square**: 1080x1080 (1:1) - Square posts
   - **Instagram Stories**: 1080x1920 (9:16) - Vertical stories
   - **YouTube**: 1920x1080 (16:9) - Standard widescreen
   - **YouTube Shorts**: 1080x1920 (9:16) - Vertical shorts
   - **Facebook**: 1920x1080 (16:9) - Widescreen posts
   - **Twitter**: 1280x720 (16:9) - Optimized for Twitter
   - **LinkedIn**: 1920x1080 (16:9) - Professional format

2. **Aspect Ratio Preview**:
   - See how your video will look after conversion
   - Check for any cropping or padding that will be applied
   - Adjust your content if needed

3. **Custom Dimensions** (Advanced):
   - Set custom width and height
   - Calculate aspect ratio automatically
   - Perfect for specific platform requirements

### Step 6: Process and Export

1. **Review Settings**:
   - Check that all subtitles are properly timed
   - Verify branding elements are positioned correctly
   - Confirm the output format is correct

2. **Start Processing**:
   - Click "Start Processing" to begin video export
   - The system will show progress through each step:
     - Initializing FFmpeg
     - Uploading video file
     - Processing subtitles
     - Adding branding elements
     - Converting aspect ratio
     - Exporting final video

3. **Download Your Video**:
   - Once processing is complete, click "Download Video"
   - The file will be saved to your downloads folder
   - Ready to upload to your chosen social platform!

## Platform-Specific Tips

### TikTok (9:16)
- **Best for**: Short, engaging content
- **Subtitle placement**: Bottom third of screen
- **Logo placement**: Top corners or bottom right
- **Content tips**: Keep text large and readable

### Instagram (1:1)
- **Best for**: Square posts and carousel content
- **Subtitle placement**: Center or bottom
- **Logo placement**: Bottom right corner
- **Content tips**: Ensure important content is in the center

### YouTube (16:9)
- **Best for**: Long-form content and tutorials
- **Subtitle placement**: Bottom third
- **Logo placement**: Bottom right corner
- **Content tips**: Use the full width effectively

### Instagram Stories (9:16)
- **Best for**: Vertical stories and highlights
- **Subtitle placement**: Center or top third
- **Logo placement**: Top corners
- **Content tips**: Keep text away from edges

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

1. **Video Won't Upload**:
   - Check file format (MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WEBM)
   - Ensure file size is under 500MB
   - Try compressing the video first

2. **Subtitles Not Appearing**:
   - Check subtitle timing (start/end times)
   - Verify text color contrasts with background
   - Ensure subtitles are within video duration

3. **Branding Not Visible**:
   - Check logo opacity (should be 70-90%)
   - Verify logo is positioned within video bounds
   - Ensure logo file is not corrupted

4. **Processing Fails**:
   - Check browser compatibility (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
   - Ensure stable internet connection
   - Try refreshing the page and starting over

### Performance Tips

1. **Optimize Video Files**:
   - Use MP4 format when possible
   - Compress large files before uploading
   - Keep resolution at 1080p or lower for faster processing

2. **Subtitle Best Practices**:
   - Keep subtitles concise (2-3 lines max)
   - Use high contrast colors
   - Avoid placing subtitles over important visual content

3. **Branding Guidelines**:
   - Keep logos small and unobtrusive
   - Use consistent branding across all videos
   - Test visibility on different devices
